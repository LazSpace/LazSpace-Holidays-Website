6 Days / 5 Nights

Majestic Kerala: Mountains to Backwaters

Munnar - Thekkady - Alleppey - Kochi

Overview

Experience the serene beauty of God's Own Country. This carefully curated 6-day journey takes you through the misty tea gardens of Munnar, the wild reserves of Thekkady, the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey on a premium houseboat, and concludes in the historic port city of Kochi.

Day-wise Plan

Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Munnar +

Arrive at Cochin International Airport (COK). Our representative will meet you and facilitate your transfer to Munnar (approx. 4 hours). Enjoy scenic waterfalls (Cheeyappara and Valara) en route. Check-in to your premium resort and relax for the evening.

Day 2: Munnar Sightseeing +

After breakfast, proceed for a full day of sightseeing. Visit the Eravikulam National Park to see the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, the Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, and Eco Point. Return to the resort for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Munnar to Thekkady +

Drive to Thekkady (Periyar) after breakfast (approx. 3.5 hours). Check-in to your jungle resort. In the afternoon, enjoy a boat safari on Periyar Lake to spot wildlife. Optional activities include spice plantation tours or Kathakali shows.

Day 4: Thekkady to Alleppey (Houseboat) +

After breakfast, depart for Alleppey (approx. 4 hours). Board a premium traditional houseboat by noon. Cruise through the serene Vembanad Lake and narrow canals. Enjoy authentic Kerala cuisine prepared fresh onboard. Overnight stay on the houseboat.

Day 5: Alleppey to Kochi +

Disembark after breakfast and drive to Kochi (approx. 2 hours). Check-in to your heritage hotel in Fort Kochi. Spend the afternoon exploring the Chinese Fishing Nets, St. Francis Church, Jewish Synagogue, and Mattancherry Palace.

Day 6: Departure from Kochi +

After breakfast, transfer to Cochin International Airport for your onward journey, bringing back beautiful memories of your Kerala holiday.
