Why Choose the Backwaters?

The shimmering backwaters of Kerala, a complex network of lagoons, lakes, and canals, represent one of the most unique ecosystems on earth. Escaping the hustle of modern life, drifting on a traditional 'Kettuvallam' (houseboat) offers profound tranquility.

According to LazSpace Holidays experts, the peak months are from October to March when the climate is spectacularly cool and pleasant.

Essential Packing List

  • Light, breathable cotton clothing
  • Eco-friendly mosquito repellent
  • A high-quality camera with zoom lens for bird watching
